Fish Catcher - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Fish Catcher ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Fish Catcher is designed to be played without requiring any personal data. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your device's camera, microphone, or photos
  • Store any gameplay data on external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

Multiplayer Features

The multiplayer mode uses direct peer-to-peer connections between players on the same network. When using multiplayer:

  • Your chosen display name is shared only with the other player in your game session
  • Your device's local IP address is used to establish the connection
  • No game data is transmitted to or stored on any external servers
  • All multiplayer data is temporary and deleted when the game session ends

Data Storage

Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Fish Catcher is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Fish Catcher does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
